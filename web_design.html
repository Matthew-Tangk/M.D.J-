Halluci Design

Halluci Design is the fictional webshop I made for a school project and where I first implemented my love for Murakami. The premise of the site was to create a webshop that showed a different type of designer clothing and also gave a platform to smaller brands to show off their work. To make it extra unique, if the creator allowed it users could even commision personalised clothes which could also be made available for sale.

Professor E

Professor E is a small designer brand based out of vietnam and a staple of avant garde fashion. To test my skills as a developer I decided I wanted to try and recreate the website as close as I could.

I got to experiment with CSS grid and making my site functional on all types of screens (desktop, tablet phone) sizes by using media queries. I also got more proficient in javascript and learned how to make hamburger menus.

You can view the site by clicking on the header.

Shia the Cannibal

Shia the Cannibal is a short adventure game in the style of old text-based RPG's. In the game you are captured by hollywood actor Shia Labeouf who turns out to secretely be a cannibal and you have to escape his dungeon.

I designed and developed this webgame myself by use of HTML, CSS and javascript. Definitely one of the tougher webapplications I have made since it required extensive javascript to work but I am very satisfied with the end result.

If you wanna play the game you can by clicking on the header.
